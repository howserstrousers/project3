ESCAPE SALON

Escape is a small salon located in Newport Beach CA that opened its doors in 2002. Escape offers an aesthetically pleasing space. Tucked away among the trees, the small converted house provides clients with a sunny backyard to lounge; either during color processing or in between services. Escape gives clients an over the top experience in hair, skin and nails. The staff at Escape is led by Patsy Howser, who has more than 30 years of experience in the hair industry. All stylists are renowned in their knowledge of hair and provide their clients with work of the highest regard. Many enjoy how they leave feeling refreshed, not having to worry about the daily upkeep. Escape is able to provide clients with a variety of hair services from simple elegant cuts to effortless natural color. Escape provides products for all kinds of hair from luxury brands; Oribe and R+Co; with Davines and Goldwell being the main color line.
